Recommended contact-us.html inquiry form attributes:

<form data-rsa-inquiry-form data-rsa-source-page="Contact Us">
  <input name="customer_name" required>
  <input name="contact_number" placeholder="0919 123 4567" required>
  <input name="email" type="email">
  <input name="subject">
  <textarea name="message"></textarea>
  <input type="hidden" name="source_page" value="Contact Us">
  <input type="hidden" name="product_id">
  <button type="submit">Submit Inquiry</button>
  <div class="rsa-api-form-status" data-rsa-form-status role="status" aria-live="polite"></div>
</form>

Required API fields:
- customer_name
- contact_number

Optional fields:
- email
- subject
- message
- source_page
- product_id
